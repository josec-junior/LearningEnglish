# Prepositions of places (04/29/2026)

The preposition ***"In"*** is used to indicate that something or someone is inside a space (like a thing in a cup or person inside a country).

Example: We're not in Las Vegas. (Nós não estamos em Las Vegas)

This prepositions is also used during periods like in the morning or in the afternoon.
- In the morning (De manhã)
- In the afternoon (De tarde/À tarde)
- In the evening (De noite)
- At night (À noite)

The preposition **"*On the left*"** is used to identify something or someone that is positioned on the left side of something (like a photo or a map).

Example: This is teacher Jo, on the left. (Esta é a professora Jo, à esquerda)

The preposition "***Next to***" means that something or someone is near other thing or other person.

Example: Rebeca is next to me. (Rebeca está ao meu lado)

The preposition "***Between***" is used when something or someone is positioned between two elements.

Example: Rebeca is between Ana and me. (Rebeca está entre Ana e mim)
