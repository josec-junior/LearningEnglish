# Questions with is and are (04/23/2026)

## Verb To Be

| Singular | Plural |
| :---: | :---: |
| I `am` | We `are` |
| You `are` | You `are` |
| He `is` | They `are`|
| She `is` | |
| It `is` | |

To make questions in English, we use the following structure:
```plaintext
[To Be Verb] + [Subject] + [Complement]
```

Is he your husband? (Ele é o seu marido?)
- No, he is my brother. (Não, ele é o meu irmão)

They are your sisters, right? (Elas são suas irmãs, certo?)
- No, they are my cousins. (Não, elas são minhas primas)

Are your parents american? (Seus pais são americanos?)
- Yes, they are from New York. (Sim, eles são de Nova Iorque)
