# Articles A - AN - THE (05/06/2026)

The articles are used to determinate nouns, the choose among them depends on the sound from the next word. The articles `a` and `an` are used only fdor singular nouns.

## Article `an`

The article `an` is used before words that starting with vowel sound.

**Example**: I'm an engineer. (Eu sou um engenheiro)

**Another Example**: Our class starts in an hour.

Though `h` is a consonant, the starting "h" in "hour" is mute, so it's a vowel sound.

## Article `a`

The article `a` is used before words that starting with consonant sound.

**Example**: I go to a university. (Eu estudo em uma universidade)

Though `u` is a vowel, the starting "u" in "university" has consonant sound (like "you").

## Article `the`

The article `the` is equivalent to the Portuguese articles "o", "a", "os", "as". It is used to indicate something specific or something that the interlocutor already know.

### Comparative

I bought a sofa. (Eu comprei um sofá. [um sofá qualquer])  
I bought the sofa. (Eu comprei o sofá. [um sofá específico que já foi mencionado anteriormente])

The article `the` also is always used in the superlative.

**Example**: Coffee is the best drink in the world. (Café é a melhor bebida do mundo)

`The` is also used to define a group of people or populations.

**Example**: The rich should help the poor. (Os ricos devem ajudar os pobres)

It is also used for countries that are collectives.

**Example**: The United States, The United Kingdom, The Netherlands.

It is also used for unique elements in the terrestrial context.

**Example**: When did people first land on the moon? (Quando o homem pisou na Lua pela primeira vez?)
