# Using Technology (05/07/2026)

## "Technology" vs "The Technology"

We use "Technology" when we are talking about technology in general or as an abstract concept.

**Example**: Technology helps us in many ways. (A tecnologia nos ajuda de muitas maneiras)

We use "The Technology" when we are referring to a specific technology, already know by the interlocutors or defined by a specific characteristic. 

**Example**: The technology in my cellphone is amazing. (A tecnologia do meu celular é incrível)

## Accessing and using the Internet

To express that we are connected, we use the term `online` and to talk about the network itself, we use `the internet`.

**Example**: I am online. (Eu estou online)

**Another Example**: I access information on the internet. (Eu acesso informações na internet)

We use the preposition `on` when we refer to actions or states inside the internet or others digital platforms.

**Example**: I saw this video on YouTube. (Eu vi esse vídeo no YouTube)

## Verbs and Collocations

The verb `listen` must always be accompanied by the preposition `to` when followed by an object.

**Example**: I listen to music. (Eu ouço música)

***Music*** is an uncountable noun in English, if you want to refer a specific music, use `song`.

**Example**: I like this song. (Eu gosto dessa música)

We use the verb `chat` to indicate an informal conversation or to exchange text messages with someone.

**Example**: I chat with my friends on the internet. (Eu converso com meus amigos pela internet)

The verb `play` can mean "jogar" or "brincar", depending on the context.

**Example**: I play games on my computer. (Eu jogo no meu computador)

**Another Example**: I play with my kids. (Eu brinco com meus filhos)
