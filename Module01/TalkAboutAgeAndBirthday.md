# Talk about age and birthday (04/24/2026)

```plaintext
Turn + [your age]
```

Example: I'm turning 24 soon. (Eu farei 24 anos em breve)

I'm from the 20s club. (Sou do clube dos 20)

Another example: She/He is turning 10 this year. (Ela/Ele completa 10 anos este ano)

**In past:** I turned 23 last month. (Eu completei 23 anos no mês passado)

What is your date of birth? (Qual é a sua data de nascimento?)
- I was born **on** March 20th, 2003. (Eu nasci em 20 de Março de 2003)

We use ON for the **complete date** and IN for only the year or only the month.

Example: I was born **in** 2003. (Eu nasci em 2003)

When is your birthday? (Quando é o seu aniversário?)
- It's on March 20th. (É dia 20 de Março)

## Years in English

To say the years in English, we can use two forms:

1. Split the numbers into two parts
    - 2026 &rarr; Twenty twenty-six.
2. Or say two thousand and the number (usually the years 2000-)
    - 2026 &rarr; Two thousand and twenty-six.

### What is your date of birth?

#### How is written in the US
- 03/20/2003 or March 20, 2003 or March 20th, 2003.

#### How is spoken in the US
- March Twentieth, Two thousand three.

#### How is written in the UK
- 20/03/2003 or 20 March 2003 or 20th March 2003.

#### How is spoken in the UK
- The Twentieth of March, Two Thousand and Three.
